Course Learning Outcomes (CLOs) in response to PLO1: Advanced Research and Development to Create New High-Impact Knowledge

       
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
CLOCLO Description
CLO 1Analyze and evaluate soil compaction phenomena by integrating the principles of soil mechanics and soil dynamics to identify complex problems with research potential.
CLO 2Design and propose research approaches for identifying and assessing the diverse effects of soil compaction, and create hypotheses or theoretical/computational models to explain the phenomena.
CLO 3Develop or improve soil compaction management strategies by deeply considering the relationship between machinery and soil compaction, and evaluate potential impacts on crop production to create innovative methods that are more highly effective than conventional methods.
CLO 4Synthesize and present work in the form of standard research papers/academic reports, capable of linking soil compaction concepts with new technologies and demonstrating the potential to create new knowledge that can lead to actual problem-solving in the agricultural industry.
